Featured Experience

Fractured Franchise

Dive into the daily chaos of a lively pizzeria. You can take on the role of a nightguard, an animatronic with advanced roleplay options, a chef whipping up a variety of dishes with a highly interactive cooking system, or a janitor keeping the place spotless. Playing jobs like chef and janitor earns in-game currency, which you can spend on characters, outfits, mini-games like prop hunt, and fun in-universe activities such as arcades and claw machines.
